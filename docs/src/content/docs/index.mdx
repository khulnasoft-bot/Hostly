Hostly is an all-in one PaaS that helps you to self-host your own applications, databases or services (like Wordpress, Plausible Analytics, Ghost) without managing your servers and all the complexity that comes with it, it is also known as an **open-source & self-hostable Heroku / Netlify / Vercel alternative**.

## Why Hostly?

### Cost Benefits
- **No Hidden Costs**: Unlike traditional cloud providers where costs can spiral once you exceed free tiers or other limitations, Hostly keeps your expenses predictable and transparent. There are plenty of <a href="https://serverlesshorrors.com" target="_blank">horror stories</a> about unexpected cloud costs.
- **Highly Cost-Efficient**: Get significantly more resources for your money. Our users regularly report saving thousands of dollars monthly compared to traditional cloud platforms - see real examples <a href="https://twitter.com/heyandras/status/1742078215986860460" target="_blank">here</a>, <a href="https://twitter.com/heyandras/status/1752209429276086688" target="_blank">here</a>, and <a href="https://twitter.com/heyandras/status/1724510876256944244" target="_blank">here</a>.

### Control & Privacy
- **Complete Data Privacy**: In an era where data privacy is crucial, Hostly ensures you maintain full control over your data and infrastructure.
- **No Feature Restrictions**: All features are included in the open-source version - no premium features behind paywalls, ever.
- **Unlimited Usage**: Deploy unlimited websites and applications across any number of servers without artificial restrictions.

### Ease of Use
- **Quick Setup**: Start hosting in minutes with minimal maintenance work required.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Manage your entire infrastructure through a clean, simple UI designed for developers.

### Community & Transparency
- **100% Open Source**: Review the code, contribute to development, and help shape the future of the platform.
- **Active Community**: Join a growing community of over 10000 people on Discord and over 85000 instances running Hostly.
